Welcome to Democratic Social Network

Social Networks should be democratic. Currently they are not, they are highly autocratic with people like emperor Zuckerberg at the top overtly censoring Facebook according to his own personal social engineering goals. Likewise, admins of groups tend to be autocrats, they are not elected or accountable in any way, can delete or block any post, comment or member with no consequences. Group members are not involved in creating or enforcing group rules. They're called social networks but let's face it, they're pretty anti-social and actually outright misanthropic. Cyber-bullying is rampant with little meaningful means of keeping it in check except in more extreme cases where police might get involved.

We deserve better, we deserve a democratic social network that actually fosters communities in a real meaningful way. One that doesn't promote low self esteem and social anxiety. One where you don't have to worry about being cancelled or ostracized for saying things that threaten powerful people, where such criticism is actively encouraged. We need a social media platform where people can actually have vigorous debates.
